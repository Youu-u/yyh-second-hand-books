Frequently Asked Questions

How to Buy a Book?

Go to the Buy page, fill out the form with your details, and select a book. After submitting, pay via PayPal or CommBank as instructed. We'll prepare your book and place it at your chosen pickup point (Nevil Wing 1st Floor Reception or NLC 1st Floor Reception).

How to Sell Your Book?

We operate on a consignment basis. Fill out the form on the Sell page with your book details and bank account info. We'll arrange a pickup on campus, assess the book's condition, and list it for sale. Once sold, you'll receive payment via bank transfer (you keep 70%, we take 30% commission).

Where Are the Pickup Points?

Books can be picked up at Nevil Wing 1st Floor Reception or NLC 1st Floor Reception. Choose your preferred location when you order.

How Can I Contact YYH?

Email us at your-school-email@school.com or message us on Teams. We're here to help!
