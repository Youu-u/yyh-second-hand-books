Frequently Asked Questions

How to Sell Your Book?

We operate on a consignment model. You submit your book details on the Sell to YYH page, and we'll arrange a campus pickup. Once your book sells, you'll receive payment via bank transfer within 5 business days. Unsold books can be returned to you upon request.

How to Buy a Book?

Go to the Buy page, fill out the form with your details, and select a book. After submitting, you'll need to pay via PayPal within 24 hours (include your Book ID in the payment note!). We'll then arrange a campus pickup.

Can I Return a Book?

We accept returns within 7 days if the book is in the same condition as purchased. Email us to arrange a return and refund.
