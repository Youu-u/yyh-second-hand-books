About Us

We are a 100% student-run non-profit organisation at Methodist Ladies’ College. Our team members are Yuhan Yang (Year 10), Aries Luo (Year 10), and Avni Hedge (Year 10).

Despite our busy academic life, we personally code the site, evaluate product prices, and handle product transfers — all to contribute to our planet’s wellbeing. We are committed to environmental protection, and all trades are conducted in an environmentally-friendly way. All profits are donated to environmental charities to help protect our Earth.

Our trading method requires no trucks and no flights — only our own hands and legs to complete product hand-overs. On campus, we provide a platform for students to trade products, and we only charge 10% service fee. Delivery is fast and produces 0 pollution.

For second-hand books, we strongly support re-use, so our service fee remains only 10% — much lower and faster than many other second-hand book platforms.

If you have any further questions, feel free to message any of us: Yuhan Yang (Year 10), Aries Luo (Year 10), Avni Hedge (Year 10).
